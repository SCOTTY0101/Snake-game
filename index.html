Snake Game

Apples are falling from the tree. Help snake to eat the health red apples and avoid the rotten brown ones.
Snake can't turn on itself but can slide over its own body.

Snake controls using the keyboard letters :
Direction 'up' is 'p'
Direction 'down' is 'l'
Direction 'left' is 'a'
Direction 'right' is 's'

Red apple is worth : 10 points and one extra body part
Gold apple is worth : 500 points and one extra body part
Brown rotten apple is : Game Over.
